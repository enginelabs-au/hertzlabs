Boilerplate (long)

Hertz Labs Binaural Beats is built for people who want control over their entrainment sessions—not pre-recorded soundscapes. The app ships a custom native audio engine (Swift on iOS) that generates binaural, monaural, isochronic, hemispheric sync, phase-modulated, and pitch-panning signals in real time, with sample-accurate low-latency playback.

Users dial exact beat frequencies on a live oscilloscope UI, explore eleven contiguous brainwave bands from infra-slow through supra-gamma, and run multi-step Protocol Sequences (focus, sleep, reset presets—or fully editable steps). Math Mode derives targets from mathematical constants; the AI Guide and Formula assistant help configure sessions conversationally, with clear safety onboarding that the app is not medical advice.

Sessions mix with Spotify, Apple Music, and other apps. Premium adds background audio (locked-screen playback), advanced engines, and the full frequency range. Published by Engine Labs; available on the App Store (iPhone; compatible with iPad and Apple Silicon Mac).
