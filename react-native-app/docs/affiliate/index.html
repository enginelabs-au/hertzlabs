Affiliate & Creator Program

Last updated: July 2026 · Engine Labs

We work with creators and marketers who promote Hertz Labs through authentic content. This is separate from in-app Refer a Friend rewards (store offer codes, no cash) and from the Practitioner track in Promos.

Apply

In the app: Feedback → Become an affiliate. Email is required. Applications go to hello@enginelabs.com.au for manual review.

If approved

Terms are agreed case by case — there is no standard public commission rate or guaranteed earnings. Depending on fit, a partner may receive referral tooling, revenue share paid outside the app, and occasionally complimentary Premium time via official subscription offer codes (same pool as Promos rewards).

Basics

  • Disclose your partnership in public content (#ad / paid partnership).
  • No paid App Store or Google Play reviews.
  • No misleading health claims or multi-level recruitment.
  • Cash payouts and tax details are handled off-app after a written agreement.

See also our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
