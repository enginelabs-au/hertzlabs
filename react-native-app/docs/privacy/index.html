Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Applies to Hertz Labs v2.0 (iOS & Android)

Overview

Hertz Labs ("we", "our", "us") is a binaural beats and audio frequency generation app. We are committed to being transparent about the limited data we collect. This policy describes what data is collected, why, and how it is handled.

Hertz Labs does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.

Data We Collect

The following table describes all categories of data that leave your device when you use Hertz Labs.

Data Processed On-Device Only

The following data is used locally and is never transmitted:

  • Motion sensor data (gyroscope, accelerometer) — used to control audio parameters in real time
  • Your audio session and frequency settings — stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
  • App preferences and state

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat — manages in-app purchases and subscriptions.
Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Google Gemini API — powers the AI Guide feature. Text prompts you enter are processed by Google.
Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Children

Hertz Labs is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13 years of age. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Data Retention

We do not maintain our own database of user data. Anonymous identifiers held by RevenueCat are subject to their retention policy. AI prompts sent to Google are subject to Google's data handling policies.

Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data linked to your identity, there is no profile to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about data held by our third-party providers, please refer to their respective privacy policies above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
